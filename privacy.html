Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Overview

AttachmentRemover ("we", "our", "the add-on") is a Gmail add-on that removes attachments from emails within your Gmail account. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we access, how we use it, and what we never do with it.

2. Data we access

To function, the add-on accesses the following data within your Google account:

  • The content and metadata of the currently open Gmail message (subject, sender, recipients, body, attachments)
  • Your Gmail labels (to create and apply the "Attachments Removed" label)
  • Your Google account email address (to identify the active user)

3. Data we do NOT collect or store

We never collect, store, transmit, or share:

  • Your email content or attachments — all processing happens inside your Google account
  • Your email address or any personally identifiable information on our servers
  • Any data with third parties, advertisers, or analytics providers

4. How your data is processed

All operations are performed entirely within your Google account using Google's own APIs. The add-on uses the Gmail REST API messages.import endpoint to insert a cleaned copy of your email directly into your mailbox. No email content ever passes through our servers.

Usage counts (number of attachments removed) are stored locally in Google's PropertiesService, which is scoped to your account. This data is never transmitted outside Google's infrastructure.

5. Third-party services

The add-on calls gmail.googleapis.com directly using your own OAuth access token. No other external services are contacted. Payment processing (for Pro licences) is handled by Stripe or Lemon Squeezy — please refer to their respective privacy policies for payment data handling.

6. OAuth permissions

The add-on requests only the Gmail OAuth scopes necessary for its core function. It does not request access to Google Drive, Contacts, Calendar, or any other Google service beyond Gmail.

7. Children's privacy

This add-on is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the add-on evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the add-on after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@attachmentremover.com.
